# evnakuru
## Intro and Hook

- Nakuru is poised for an EV revolution, but here's the harsh truth:
- An estimated 75% of potential EV owners in the region are holding back due to a lack of reliable charging infrstructure.
- This hesitation could cost Nakuru's economic development and green job creation. 
- We're uncovering the hidden challenges and groundbreaking solutions that can turn this tide, and fast.
